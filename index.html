1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information

  • Name: Used for user profiles, matchmaking, and event participation.
  • Email Address: Used for account authentication, notifications, and event confirmations.
  • Phone Number: Used for authentication (via Firebase) and event communication.
  • Date of Birth: Used to verify age eligibility for events.

B. Payment Information

We do not store your credit or debit card details. Payments are processed securely through Stripe, which handles transactions and compliance. Stripe may collect payment details, billing addresses, and other necessary information per their Privacy Policy.

C. Event and Matchmaking Data

We collect preferences such as age range and gender preference for matchmaking, as well as event participation details.

D. Location Data

If you grant permission, we collect your location to show nearby events and improve matchmaking. Location data is only used while the app is running or as allowed by your device settings. You can disable location tracking at any time through your device settings.

E. Device and Usage Data

Firebase Analytics may collect non-personal usage data, such as device type, app interactions, and crash reports. Firebase Cloud Messaging may collect notification tokens to send event reminders and updates.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Enable core app functionality, including user authentication, matchmaking, and event sign-ups.
  • Process payments securely through Stripe.
  • Send notifications about matches, events, and updates through Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • Provide location-based event recommendations and matchmaking.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security of our platform.
  • Provide customer support.

We do not sell or share your data for third-party advertising.

3. How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: Secure data transmission and storage.
  • Authentication Controls: Firebase Authentication to protect access.
  • Stripe Security Standards: PCI-compliant payment processing.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third parties:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Authentication, database storage, analytics, and notifications.
  • Stripe: Secure payment processing.
  • Apple (for App Store compliance): Manages in-app transactions if applicable.

  • Opt-out of notifications in your device settings.
